### Step 1: 全面定义

- **读音**：/bentʃ/
  - 英国英语和美国英语的读音都是 /bentʃ/，但在具体的发音上可能略有差别。

- **定义**：
  1. **A long seat for several people**: 
     - 英文定义：A long seat typically found in parks or public spaces, designed to accommodate several people.
     - 中文翻译：供多人坐的长椅，通常位于公园或公共场所。
  2. **A judge's seat in a courtroom**:
     - 英文定义：The seat where a judge sits in a courtroom.
     - 中文翻译：法官在法庭上坐的席位。
  3. **A workbench in a workshop or laboratory**:
     - 英文定义：A sturdy table used by carpenters, mechanics, and researchers for manual work.
     - 中文翻译：木工、机械师和研究人员用于手工作业的坚固桌子。
  4. **In sports, a place where substitute players sit**:
     - 英文定义：The area where players who are not currently participating in the game wait.
     - 中文翻译：不参与当前比赛的球员等候的区域。

### Step 2: 精确的语境化例句

1. **As a long seat**:
   - 使用场景：在公园里的常见情况。
   - 例句：We sat on the bench and watched the ducks in the pond.
  
2. **As a judge's seat**:
   - 使用场景：法庭上的情境。
   - 例句：The judge took his place on the bench to deliver the verdict.

3. **As a workbench**:
   - 使用场景：车间或实验室内。
   - 例句：She spread the blueprints out on the bench to examine the design.

4. **As a place for substitute athletes**:
   - 使用场景：在体育比赛中。
   - 例句：The coach called him off the bench to replace the injured player.

### Step 3: 词汇的亲属

- **Synonyms**:
  - **Seat**: A term for a place to sit; generally more generic than "bench".
  - **Workstation**: Specifically used for workplaces, particularly with computers involved; usually more specialized than "bench".
  
- **Antonyms**:
  - **Standing area**: An area where people stand.
  
- **Derived Words**:
  - **Workbench**: A bench used for work, emphasizing its function.
  
- **Related Compounds**:
  - **Bench press**: A weightlifting exercise.

### Step 4: 词源探索

- **词源**：
  - “Bench”源于古英语“benc”，与德语“Bank”同源，原意是指“长凳”或“平台”。

### Step 1: 全面定义

**英文定义**：  
**Toothache** (noun):  
1. **Primary Meaning**: A pain in one or more teeth, typically caused by dental issues such as cavities, tooth decay, gum disease, or injury.  
   - 中文翻译：牙痛，一种由牙齿问题（如蛀牙、牙齿腐烂、牙龈疾病或受伤）引起的牙齿疼痛。

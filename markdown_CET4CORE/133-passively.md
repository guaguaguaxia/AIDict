### Step 1: 全面定义

**英文定义与中文翻译**  
**Passively** 是一个副词，派生自形容词 *passive*，主要描述以“被动”或“消极”的方式进行某事。以下是其常见含义的英文定义及中文翻译：

1. **Meaning 1: In a way that lacks action or initiative; accepting or submitting without resistance**  
   - **英文定义**: Acting in a way that shows a lack of active response or resistance, often allowing things to happen without taking initiative or control.  
   - **中文翻译**: 以被动的、缺乏主动性的方式；接受或屈从而不反抗。  
   - **例**: Someone who passively accepts criticism does not argue or defend themselves but takes it without response.

2. **Meaning 2: In a way that involves receiving rather than initiating action (often in technical or grammatical contexts)**  
   - **英文定义**: Describing an action or process where the subject is acted upon rather than acting, such as in the passive voice in grammar or in technical processes like passive cooling.  
   - **中文翻译**: 以被动的、接受的方式（常用于技术或语法语境，如被动语态或被动冷却）。  
   - **例**: In grammar, a sentence is written passively when the subject receives the action (e.g., "The book was read by her").

3. **Meaning 3: In a way that shows indifference or lack of engagement**  
   - **英文定义**: Acting with a lack of interest, enthusiasm, or emotional involvement, often appearing detached or uninvolved.  
   - **中文翻译**: 以冷漠的、缺乏参与感的方式；显得疏远或不投入。  
   - **例**: Watching TV passively suggests the viewer is not actively engaging with the content but letting it play in the background.
